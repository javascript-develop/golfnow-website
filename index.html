Convenience fee

Author: GolfNow

Convenience fees are charged for all tee times booked through the GolfNow website and mobile app. Convenience fees have a broad range depending on location and are charged per player, per tee time and are reflected in the total price on your tee time reservation at the time of checkout. As a GolfPass+member, convenience fees will be waived on your first 10 bookings (up to 4 players per booking) on GolfNow.com and the GolfNow mobile app per year of your membership. Members must book all tee time reservations while signed into the same account used to purchase the GolfPass+ membership in order to receive the waived convenience fees benefit at checkout. Other taxes may apply on tee time reservations. The Waived Convenience Fees benefit will be applied at the time that a qualified reservation is made. As a GolfPass+ VIP member, convenience fees will be waived on all your bookings on GolfNow.com or GolfNow mobile app.Members must book all tee time reservations while signed into the same account used to purchase the GolfPass+ VIP membership in order to receive the waived convenience fees benefit at checkout. Weather policy

Author: GolfNow

Any unplayed reservation can be cancelled and refunded if the golf course is closed on the day of play. To cancel a reservation due to weather, go to your View Reservations page and click “Modify Players or Cancel” on the reservation you would like to cancel. Select the number of players you wish to remove from your reservation and select "Weather" for the reason of cancellation, then click “Submit Cancellation.” If you are eligible for a refund, please allow 5-7 business days for processing. Hot Deals reservations are non-refundable once your round has begun. Tee time reservations paid at the golf course adhere to the golf course’s rain check policy once the round has begun. Please see the golf course pro shop to be issued a potential rain check. GolfPass+ members are covered under Cancellation Protection tee times, which allows GolfPass+ members to cancel or modify their tee time reservation online at least one (1) hour prior to their scheduled tee time, for any reason, without penalty, up to ten (10) times per year of their membership. GolfPass+ members will receive a GolfNow credit equivalent to the green fees due for the modified or cancelled tee time, which will be automatically applied to the member's GolfNow account and can be found under Account Payment options.  These credits can be used toward a future Hot Deal booking
